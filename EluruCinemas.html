Details of SVC in CMR Central

SVC Cinemas at CMR Mall, Eluru is a multiplex near the New Bus Stand on Vijayawada Road, featuring 4K projection with Dolby Atmos sound, multiple daily showtimes for Telugu and Hindi films, online booking through major platforms, and amenities like air‑conditioning, parking, digital payments, F&B, wheelchair‑friendly access, and some recliner seating; typical seat categories include Gold and premium options, with prices varying by movie and timing—check the day’s schedule before visiting.

Details of sri-satyanarayana-cinemas-benerjipeta-eluru-cinema-halls

Sri Satyanarayana Cinemas: Located on Eastern Street, Paidichintapadu, Eluru (also sometimes referenced in Benerjipeta), this is a classic local cinema screening a variety of regional and popular releases. It typically offers multiple showtimes across the day for new Telugu and Hindi films. Ticket booking is available online and at the theatre, with typical pricing brackets including Floor, Bench, and Balcony categories. The theatre is well-known locally but may have more basic amenities compared to modern multiplexes.

Details of the theatres

Details of sai-balaji-eluru-cinema-halls

Sai Balaji Cinema Hall: Situated in Main Bazar, near Gopala Krishna Theatre, Agraharam, this theatre complex is centrally located and popular for regional cinema, including daily showings of Telugu movies. It is known for its accessibility, multiple showtime slots, and price categories such as Third Class, Second Class, and First Class (with prices like ₹50, ₹80, and ₹150 respectively). Online booking is supported by platforms like TicketNew and Paytm.

Details of Ambica-eluru-cinema-halls

Ambica Deluxe / Ambica Complex: Found on Western Street or in Agraharam, 1 Town, Eluru, this long-running single-screen theatre is a legacy venue for local audiences. With a seating capacity of around 383, it hosts a variety of movie formats and maintains a strong neighborhood presence. Amenities prioritize broad accessibility and value, with online ticketing widely available.

Details of V-Max-eluru-cinema-halls

V Max Theatre: Located at Thoorpu Veedhi, Eastern Street, Eluru, V Max is a well-rated cinema with a 3.8/5 rating from over 4,000 reviews. It features wheelchair accessible entrances and parking, restaurant and restroom amenities, and is considered family and kid-friendly. The theatre is known for its quality projection (A/C 2K Dolby setup), food options, and cash payments.
